That covered running existing functions with your choice of keystrokes. What if you want your own functions? What if you are passive aggressive and want to use C-F to type out, "My boss has pointy hair. Please _ me." and have the cursor come back to the blank after all the letters are displayed? There are two ways to do this. If you don't have a thick beard, glasses, and a hideously stained coffee mug you can use a macro to create the necessary function for you. If you are holding a pale yellow / brown coffee mug that was once white, and you have a beard and glasses, you are probably not reading this, instead you are writing the function directly into you .emacs.d/init.el file.
